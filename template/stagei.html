Sample Survey Questions

After Workshop...

1)Was the program good and useful?


2)Did you understand most of the topics/information shared?


3)Did you enjoy the activities?


4)Are the presentations / videos/work materials good to see, read and easy to understand?


5)Has your knowledge increased about different career paths / job opportunities / preparation?


6)Do you have better clarity now on your next steps than before the program?


7)Do you recommend this program for your juniors / other friends too?


8)Will you reach out to us if you have questions?


9)Is it easy for you to set your own goals now? Can you do it?


10)Has your career choices changed with new or more options now?


11)Are you more aware and confident about your career choices as well as decision making now?

12)Which part of the session you enjoyed the most?
